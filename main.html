Here is a bio section that totally describes who I am. It is actually a few paragraphs and over 300 words long!

Despite the fact that I initially knew very little about programming and computers in general, I really want to be a computer engineer. I was previously a pharmacy student but left pharmacy school because I did not like what I was learning, and I did not like learning in an environment in which the most difficult mathematical knowledge required was basic algebra. I wanted something more mentally engaging, and with so many non-pharmacy interests, a curriculum focused solely upon the inculcation of pharmacy knowledge was a real tedium for me. After having known so many computer nerds and a few actual (i.e. non-student) computer engineers and seeing what they do, I realized that computer engineering might be a much better fit for me; taking a programming class confirmed that. Now, I am actually a super senior, but in terms of academic progress I am a ridiculously old junior..

My parents didn't like the idea of me becoming an engineer.
"But-but-but pharmacy is such a good career for girls!" my mother said, "which is why I decided that you should become a pharmacist when you were too young to make that decision for yourself!"
"Engineering is for boys!" my father said.
"Those men are going to push you around and abuse you!" they said.

And then I moved out despite the fact that I have absolutely no money because dying of starvation is completely worth not spending the rest of my life as a self-loathing pharmacist. For a while, I had to subsist on mostly potatoes, onions, and bananas until I could find a job, which was difficult because I had no non-pharmacy skills and could no longer work as a pharmacy intern.

My grandparents rise from the dead every All Hallows' Eve to feed candy to all my friends. The pattern of growth of Romanesco broccoli is nothing like a fractal at all. Unicorns live in my navel. Spending too much time on Reddit is always fun. None of this paragraph is true.

I like cats and lattes.

I have terrible humor.
